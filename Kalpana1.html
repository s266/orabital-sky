KALPANA 1

About Kalpana 1

First meteorological satellite built by Indian Space Research Organization.

Kalpana - 1 was the first dedicated meteorological satellite. It was launched by Indian Space Research Organisation using the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle on 12 September 2002.

The satellite is three-axis stabilized and is powered by solar panels, getting up to 550 watts (0.74 hp) of power.
