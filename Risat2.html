About Risat-2

It was built as part of an acceleration of Indian reconnaissance

satellite procurement after the 2008 Mumbai (Bombay) terror attacks, and due to delays with the indigenously developed RISAT-1 spacecraft, which is due for launch in 2011.

Hence, RISAT-2 is India's first satellite with a synthetic aperture radar (SAR), which possesses a 24-hour, all-weather monitoring capability.
